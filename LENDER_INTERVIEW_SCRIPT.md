# Internal Lender Interview Script
**Purpose:** Capture tribal knowledge for Base44 Mortgage Assistant knowledge base
**Duration:** 10-15 minutes per lender
**Format:** Zoom (video preferred for transcription)
**Deadline:** 1 week

**Interviewer:** Tara (or designated interviewer)
**Interviewees:** Nwabisa and Dextter (conduct separate sessions for each)
**Process:** One interview per lender, per broker (expect repetition, but each broker may have different insights)

---

## Pre-Interview Setup

**Interviewer says to Nwabisa/Dextter:**
- "Which lender are we covering today?"
- "How long have you been working with them?"
- "Roughly how many cases have you placed with them in the last 12 months?"

**Context for Nwabisa/Dextter:**
*"We're building a knowledge base for our mortgage assistant AI. The goal is to capture the stuff that's NOT on their website - the quirks, the unwritten rules, the things you've learned from experience. Think: What would you tell a new broker about this lender? I'm going to ask you the same questions for each lender - it'll be repetitive, but we need your specific insights for each one."*

---

## Section 1: Relationship & Communication (2 mins)

### 1.1 BDM & Support
- **Who is our main BDM contact?** (Name, phone, email, responsiveness rating 1-10)
- **How responsive is their underwriting team?** (Typical response time for queries)
- **Do they prefer phone calls, email, or portal messages?**
- **Any particular quirks about how they like to be contacted?**
  *(e.g., "Don't call on Mondays", "Always CC the BDM", "Use the portal for everything")*

### 1.2 Submission Preferences
- **Do they have any pet hates when receiving cases?**
  *(e.g., incomplete docs, wrong file formats, missing info in cover notes)*
- **What do they LOVE to see in a submission?**
  *(e.g., detailed cover notes, pre-empted queries, specific document labeling)*

---

## Section 2: Criteria Deep Dive (5-6 mins)

### 2.1 Income Assessment
- **Self-employed applicants:** What's their ACTUAL appetite vs what the website says?
  *(e.g., "They say 1 year, but really need 2 years trading", "They're flexible on retained profits if strong accountant letter")*
- **Complex income (bonuses, commission, overtime):** How do they assess it?
  *(e.g., "They average 2 years", "They ignore first year if <6 months employed", "They take 50% of bonus")*
- **Contractors:** What do they actually accept?
  *(e.g., "Day rate x 46 weeks", "Need 12 months history", "CIS acceptable?")*
- **Gaps in employment:** How lenient are they? Any red lines?

### 2.2 Credit & Affordability
- **Adverse credit:** What's their TRUE appetite?
  *(e.g., "Website says no defaults, but they'll consider if >3 years old and <Â£500")*
- **CCJs/Defaults:** Maximum number, value, and age they'll realistically consider?
- **Debt-to-income ratio:** What's their actual threshold? Any flexibility?
- **Student loans:** Do they include in affordability? Plan 1 vs Plan 2 differences?
- **Gambling/payday loans on bank statements:** Immediate decline or case-by-case?

### 2.3 Property & LTV
- **Non-standard construction:** What do they actually accept?
  *(e.g., "Ex-local authority is fine", "They'll do flats above commercial but need manual review", "No timber frame above 75% LTV")*
- **Flats:** Do they have floor limits? Cladding concerns? Purpose-built vs conversion?
- **New builds:** Any restrictions on LTV or developers? Retention issues?
- **Rural properties:** Do they have acreage limits? Septic tanks OK?
- **Leasehold:** Minimum lease term remaining? Ground rent limits (post-2022 legislation)?

### 2.4 Product Nuances
- **Rate switching:** Can existing customers switch products mid-term?
- **Portability:** How smooth is their porting process? Any horror stories?
- **Overpayments:** True limits? Any penalties beyond the 10% standard?
- **Early Repayment Charges:** Are they negotiable for partial redemptions?

### 2.5 Specialist Scenarios (Ask if time allows)
- **Expats/Foreign Nationals:** What documentation do they actually need? Any nationality restrictions?
- **First-Time Buyers:** Do they have specific FTB products? Gifted deposits OK?
- **Right-to-Buy:** Immediate remortgage OK, or do they want seasoning?
- **Shared Ownership:** Do they lend on it? Staircasing restrictions?
- **Help-to-Buy equity loans:** Will they lend alongside HTB? Any LTV restrictions?
- **Retirement interest-only (RIO):** Age limits? Income assessment approach?
- **Joint borrower sole proprietor (JBSP):** Do they offer it? Income multiples?

---

## Section 3: Underwriting Reality (3 mins)

### 3.1 Processing Times
- **What's their REAL turnaround time right now?**
  *(e.g., "Website says 5 days, but allow 10-14 days currently due to backlog")*
- **Mortgage in Principle (MIP):** Instant? Manual? Worth bothering with?
- **Valuation turnaround:** Typical wait time?
- **Final offer to completion:** How long does post-valuation underwriting take?

### 3.2 Underwriter Behavior
- **Are their underwriters risk-averse or pragmatic?**
  *(e.g., "They're box-tickers", "They use common sense", "They're terrified of FCA")*
- **Do they come back with endless queries or make decisions quickly?**
- **Any known underwriter 'personalities'?**
  *(e.g., "John is tough on self-employed", "Sarah is great with complex cases")*
- **Do they decline-by-stealth (endless conditions) or give a straight 'no'?**

### 3.3 Documentation Pet Peeves
- **What documents do they ALWAYS ask for, even if not listed on checklist?**
  *(e.g., "They always want 3 months business bank statements", "They ask for proof of deposit source even at 60% LTV")*
- **Any specific formatting requirements?**
  *(e.g., "Bank statements must show full account number", "Payslips must be signed by employer")*
- **Do they accept digital signatures? ID verification via app?**

### 3.4 Valuation Quirks
- **Which panel do they use?** *(e.g., "Mix of LSL/e.surv/Countrywide")*
- **Do they down-value frequently?** *(Conservative vs optimistic)*
  *(e.g., "They ALWAYS undervalue by 5-10%", "They're realistic")*
- **Any property types they struggle to value?**
  *(e.g., "Rural properties always need re-inspection", "Flats are fine but houses take ages")*
- **Retention policies:** Do they hold back funds for minor issues?
  *(e.g., "They'll retain Â£5k for damp course", "They're pragmatic - completion monies released")*
- **Re-inspection process:** Quick or painful?

---

## Section 4: Red Flags & Deal-Breakers (2 mins)

### 4.1 Instant Declines
- **What will they NEVER accept, regardless of case strength?**
  *(e.g., "No HMOs", "No ex-pats", "No right-to-buy within 5 years", "No interest-only for first-time buyers")*
- **Any applicant profiles they quietly avoid?**
  *(e.g., "They say they do self-employed, but really prefer PAYE", "They hate company directors")*

### 4.2 Geography
- **Any postcode restrictions?**
  *(e.g., "They won't touch Liverpool", "They red-flag certain estates", "Scotland is manual referral only")*
- **Do they have property price floors/ceilings?**
  *(e.g., "Nothing under Â£50k", "Nothing over Â£1m without manual UW")*

---

## Section 5: Competitive Edge (2 mins)

### 5.1 When to Use This Lender
- **What type of case is this lender PERFECT for?**
  *(e.g., "Best for self-employed with 1 year accounts", "Go-to for flats above shops", "King of 95% LTV")*
- **When would you AVOID using them, even if they fit criteria?**
  *(e.g., "If client needs speed", "If case has ANY complexity", "If client is high-maintenance")*

### 5.2 Comparison to Competitors
- **Who is their closest competitor, and how do they differ?**
  *(e.g., "Similar to Lender X but faster", "Cheaper rates than Lender Y but stricter on credit")*
- **If this lender declines, who do you typically go to next?**

### 5.3 Rate & Fee Reality
- **Are their rates ACTUALLY competitive, or is it a bait-and-switch?**
  *(e.g., "Great headline rate but massive fees", "Rate looks high but underwriting is fast so saves time")*
- **What are their typical arrangement fees? Any hidden costs?**
- **Do they negotiate on proc fees for introducers/networks?**
- **Cashback/incentives: Are they real or full of conditions?**

---

## Section 6: Recent Changes & Trends (1 min)

### 6.1 Market Shifts
- **Have they tightened or loosened criteria in the last 6 months?**
  *(e.g., "They've gone cold on interest-only", "They're now doing 5.5x income instead of 4.5x")*
- **Any product changes or new offerings?**
  *(e.g., "New green mortgage cashback", "Stopped doing buy-to-let")*
- **Are they appetite-hunting or pulling back right now?**

### 6.2 Pandemic/Economic Hangover
- **How did they behave during COVID? Any trust issues?**
  *(e.g., "They withdrew products overnight", "They were rock-solid throughout")*
- **Are they nervous about interest rate changes? Tightening as a result?**

### 6.3 Tech & Portal Experience
- **How good is their broker portal?** (1-10 rating)
  *(e.g., "Clunky and crashes", "Smooth and intuitive", "Mobile-friendly?")*
- **Can you track application progress in real-time?**
- **Do they have an API or integration options?** *(Future-proofing for Base44)*
- **Digital ID verification: Do they use it? Which provider?**
- **E-signatures accepted? Wet signatures still required for anything?**

---

## Section 7: War Stories (1 min)

### 7.1 Best Case
- **Tell me about a case that went unexpectedly WELL with this lender.**
  *(Capture why - flexible underwriter, commercial approach, saved a tricky case, etc.)*

### 7.2 Worst Case
- **Tell me about a case that went unexpectedly BADLY with this lender.**
  *(Capture why - unreasonable decline, moved goalposts, poor communication, etc.)*

### 7.3 Edge Cases & Gotchas
- **Any recent cases where they surprised you (positively or negatively)?**
- **What's the weirdest reason they've declined a case?**
- **Any "hidden" criteria not on their website that you've discovered the hard way?**
  *(e.g., "They auto-decline if applicant changed jobs in last 6 months", "They won't touch anyone with Revolut as main bank account")*

---

## Section 8: Client Experience (1 min)

### 8.1 Post-Completion
- **How are they with customer service once mortgage completes?**
  *(e.g., "Impossible to reach", "Great retention team", "Annual statements are clear")*
- **Do clients complain about them post-completion?**
  *(e.g., "Direct debit issues", "Poor communication", "Good online servicing")*
- **Redemption process: Smooth or painful?**

### 8.2 Reputation
- **Would you recommend them to a friend/family member?** (Honest answer)
- **Do clients ever specifically REQUEST this lender?** (Brand strength indicator)
- **Any reputational red flags we should know about?**
  *(e.g., "Bad reviews online", "FCA investigations", "Complaints about exit fees")*

---

## Closing Questions

- **If you could change ONE thing about this lender, what would it be?**
- **On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to use them again?**
- **Anything else we should know that I haven't asked about?**
- **What question should I have asked that I didn't?** *(Uncovers blindspots)*

---

## Post-Interview Actions

1. **Transcribe** (Zoom auto-transcript or manual)
2. **Save to Notion** under Lenders database (create page per lender)
3. **Tag** with: Lender name, interviewer, date, interviewee
4. **Cross-reference** with existing lender criteria docs
5. **Flag any contradictions** between website info and interview insights
6. **Extract quantitative data** for Base44 decision engine:
   - Max LTV per scenario
   - Income multiples (employed vs self-employed)
   - Max adverse credit (number, value, age)
   - Processing time ranges (MIP, valuation, offer, completion)
   - Fees (arrangement, valuation, proc)
7. **Identify decision tree triggers** *(e.g., "IF self-employed AND <2 years trading THEN exclude Lender X")*
8. **Create lender comparison matrix data** *(For "show me all lenders that accept X" queries)*

## Notes for Interviewer (Tara)

- **Don't rush.** If Nwabisa/Dextter starts a good story, let them talk.
- **Probe on vague answers.** If they say "they're OK with adverse," ask for specifics (numbers, thresholds, examples).
- **Capture exact quotes.** Phrases like "they always..." or "never..." are gold.
- **Ask for examples.** Real case numbers (anonymized) add credibility.
- **Note tone.** If they groan or light up mentioning a lender, that's data.
- **Don't worry about repetition.** Nwabisa and Dextter may have different experiences with the same lender - capture both perspectives.
- **Compare notes after.** If Nwabisa says "they're fast" and Dextter says "they're slow," that's worth investigating (different BDMs? Different case types?).
