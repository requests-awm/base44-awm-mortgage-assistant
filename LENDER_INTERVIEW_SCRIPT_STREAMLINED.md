# Rapid-Fire Lender Interview Script (50+ Lender Sessions)

**Purpose:** Capture tribal knowledge for Base44 decision engine
**Duration:** 8-10 minutes per lender
**Format:** Zoom (video for transcription) or rapid note-taking
**Context:** Repetitive process - same questions for 50+ lenders
**Pre-requisite:** Broker has completed pre-interview Google Form

---

## Pre-Interview Prep (2 mins)

**Tara reviews:**
- Broker's form responses (note extreme ratings)
- Lender's website criteria page (open in browser tab)
- Notion template ready for rapid note-taking

**No conversational opening needed** - broker knows the drill after first session.

---

## Opening (15 seconds)

**First session only:**
"We're doing 50+ lenders, same questions each time. Quick fire - I'll ask, you answer. No need to elaborate unless it's critical. Ready?"

**All subsequent sessions:**
"[Lender name]. Go."

---

## Section 1: Communication & Submission (1 min)

**Q1:** "Preferred contact method - phone, email, portal?"
- [Capture: Phone/Email/Portal + any quirks]

**Q2:** "What annoys their underwriters?"
- [Capture: Submission pet peeves - format, missing docs, etc.]

**Q3:** "What impresses them?"
- [Capture: What gets cases fast-tracked]

---

## Section 2: Criteria Reality (3-4 mins)

**Context:** "Website vs reality - give me the ACTUAL thresholds."

### Income
**Q4:** "Self-employed - 1 year or 2 years minimum trading?"
- [Capture: Actual requirement + retained profit vs salary approach]

**Q5:** "Complex income - how do they calculate bonuses/commission/overtime?"
- [Capture: Average 2 years? Percentage? Ignore first year if low?]

**Q6:** "Contractors - day rate formula? CIS accepted?"
- [Capture: Formula (e.g., day rate x 46 weeks) + CIS yes/no]

### Credit
**Q7:** "Adverse credit - max CCJs/defaults (number, value, age)?"
- [Capture: e.g., "Max 2 CCJs, <Â£500 each, >3 years old"]

**Q8:** "Gambling or payday loans on statements - instant decline?"
- [Capture: Yes/No/Case-by-case]

**Q9:** "Student loans in affordability? Plan 1 vs Plan 2?"
- [Capture: Yes/No + distinction]

### Property
**Q10:** "Non-standard construction - what do they actually accept?"
- [Capture: Ex-local authority OK? Flats above commercial? Timber frame LTV limits?]

**Q11:** "Flats - floor limits? Cladding/EWS1 requirements?"
- [Capture: Max floor + EWS1 auto-required?]

**Q12:** "Leasehold - minimum lease term? Ground rent limits enforced?"
- [Capture: Years required + legislation compliance]

**Q13:** "Postcode restrictions - any areas they avoid?"
- [Capture: Specific postcodes or regions]

---

## Section 3: Underwriting Behavior (1-2 mins)

**Q14:** "Underwriters - box-tickers or common sense? Decline-by-stealth or straight 'no'?"
- [Capture: Risk appetite + decision style]

**Q15:** "What docs do they ALWAYS ask for that aren't on the checklist?"
- [Capture: e.g., business bank statements, proof of deposit source at low LTV]

**Q16:** "Digital signatures accepted? ID verification method?"
- [Capture: Yes/No + app name or wet signature required]

**Q17:** "Valuations - conservative or realistic? Any property types problematic?"
- [Capture: Down-value frequently? Struggle with rural/flats/etc.]

**Q18:** "Retention policies - pragmatic or cautious?"
- [Capture: Hold back funds for minor issues? Re-inspection painful?]

---

## Section 4: Instant Declines (30 seconds)

**Q19:** "What will they NEVER accept - applicant types, property types, scenarios?"
- [Capture: e.g., HMOs, right-to-buy <5 years, company directors, interest-only FTB]

**Q20:** "Website says 'yes' but reality is 'no' - any examples?"
- [Capture: Criteria that look flexible but aren't]

---

## Section 5: Competitive Edge (1 min)

**Q21:** "Perfect for what case type?" (from form - clarify if needed)
- [Capture: Specific use case]

**Q22:** "If they decline, who's your backup lender?"
- [Capture: Alternative lender name]

**Q23:** "Rates/fees - competitive or bait-and-switch? Hidden costs?"
- [Capture: Real vs advertised + cashback conditions]

---

## Section 6: Recent Changes (30 seconds)

**Q24:** "Last 6 months - tightened or loosened criteria? Product changes?"
- [Capture: Income multiples changed? Products withdrawn? Appetite shift?]

**Q25:** "Portal issues - what's broken or frustrating?" (from form rating)
- [Capture: Crashes, slow, can't track progress, etc.]

---

## Section 7: War Stories (2 mins) **[CRITICAL - DON'T SKIP]**

**Q26:** "Best case story - what went unexpectedly well and why?"
- [Let them talk - capture WHY it worked]

**Q27:** "Worst case story - what went wrong and why?"
- [Let them talk - capture WHY it failed]

**Q28:** "Weirdest decline reason?"
- [Capture: Edge cases]

**Q29:** "Hidden gotchas not on their website?"
- [Capture: e.g., "Auto-decline if job change <6 months", "Won't accept Revolut as main bank"]

---

## Section 8: Client Experience (30 seconds)

**Q30:** "Post-completion - clients complain? Redemption smooth or painful?"
- [Capture: Customer service quality + redemption process]

**Q31:** "Would you recommend to family? Do clients request them by name?"
- [Capture: Yes/No + brand strength]

---

## Closing (30 seconds)

**Q32:** "One thing you'd change about this lender?"
- [Capture: Biggest pain point]

**Q33:** "Anything critical I didn't ask about?"
- [Capture: Blindspot insights]

---

## Post-Interview Actions (Tara - 3 mins)

1. **Flag key quotes** - phrases like "they always..." or "never..." (decision-tree gold)
2. **Paste notes into Notion template** (structured below)
3. **Extract binary rules** for Base44 (e.g., "IF X THEN exclude")
4. **Move to next lender** (repeat)

## Tips for Tara (High-Volume Sessions)

### Efficiency Rules
- **No elaboration unless critical** - capture answer, move on
- **War stories section is non-negotiable** - this is where the gold is
- **Vague answers:** Push back with "Give me a number" or "Give me an example"
- **If they ramble:** Cut in with "Got it - moving on to Q[X]"
- **If they forget:** Note "[Broker to email details]" and move on

### Session Flow
- **First session:** 10-12 mins (they'll be slower)
- **Sessions 5+:** 6-8 mins (they know the drill)
- **Back-to-back sessions:** Max 5 lenders per hour with 2-min breaks

### Capture Priorities (in order)
1. **Binary rules** - "They NEVER accept X" or "They ALWAYS require Y"
2. **Numeric thresholds** - "Max 2 CCJs", "<Â£500", ">3 years old"
3. **War story lessons** - Why cases succeeded or failed
4. **Hidden criteria** - Website doesn't mention, but they decline for it

### Quality Over Speed
- Don't sacrifice war stories (Q26-29) for speed
- If time pressure, skip Q24-25 (recent changes/portal issues)
- Never skip Q19-20 (instant declines) or Q7-13 (criteria thresholds)

## Key Changes from Original

### Removed
- All conversational padding and context-setting
- Probe questions (brokers know what detail to provide after first session)
- Redundant clarifications
- Pre-amble explanations

### Added
- Question numbering (Q1-Q33) for rapid reference
- Structured capture format [brackets] for note-taking
- Time estimates per section for pacing
- Throughput calculations for planning

### Why This Works for 50+ Sessions
- **Repetition builds efficiency** - by session 5, brokers auto-provide the right level of detail
- **Numbered questions** - Tara can say "Q14" instead of re-reading the question
- **Form pre-populates quantitative data** - interview is 100% qualitative insights
- **War stories prioritized** - most valuable section is protected even if time pressured
- **Notion template matches question numbers** - rapid copy-paste of notes
