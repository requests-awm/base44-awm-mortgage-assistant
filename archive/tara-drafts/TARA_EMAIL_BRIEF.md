### **Step 3: Book & Conduct Interviews (Day 4-14)**

Schedule 10-15 min Zoom interviews for each lender they selected.

**Interview Questions (Q1-Q33):**

**Section 1: Communication (1 min)**
- Q1: Preferred contact method - phone, email, portal?
- Q2: What annoys their underwriters?
- Q3: What impresses them?

**Section 2: Criteria Reality (3-4 mins)**
- Q4: Self-employed - 1 year or 2 years minimum trading?
- Q5: Complex income - how do they calculate bonuses/commission/overtime?
- Q6: Contractors - day rate formula? CIS accepted?
- Q7: Adverse credit - max CCJs/defaults (number, value, age)?
- Q8: Gambling or payday loans on statements - instant decline?
- Q9: Student loans in affordability? Plan 1 vs Plan 2?
- Q10: Non-standard construction - what do they actually accept?
- Q11: Flats - floor limits? Cladding/EWS1 requirements?
- Q12: Leasehold - minimum lease term? Ground rent limits enforced?
- Q13: Postcode restrictions - any areas they avoid?

**Section 3: Underwriting Behavior (1-2 mins)**
- Q14: Underwriters - box-tickers or common sense? Decline-by-stealth or straight 'no'?
- Q15: What docs do they ALWAYS ask for that aren't on the checklist?
- Q16: Digital signatures accepted? ID verification method?
- Q17: Valuations - conservative or realistic? Any property types problematic?
- Q18: Retention policies - pragmatic or cautious?

**Section 4: Instant Declines (30 secs)**
- Q19: What will they NEVER accept - applicant types, property types, scenarios?
- Q20: Website says 'yes' but reality is 'no' - any examples?

**Section 5: Competitive Edge (1 min)**
- Q21: Perfect for what case type? (from form - clarify if needed)
- Q22: If they decline, who's your backup lender?
- Q23: Rates/fees - competitive or bait-and-switch? Hidden costs?

**Section 6: Recent Changes (30 secs)**
- Q24: Last 6 months - tightened or loosened criteria? Product changes?
- Q25: Portal issues - what's broken or frustrating?

**Section 7: War Stories (2 mins) - THIS IS THE GOLD**
- Q26: Best case story - what went unexpectedly well and why?
- Q27: Worst case story - what went wrong and why?
- Q28: Weirdest decline reason?
- Q29: Hidden gotchas not on their website?

**Section 8: Client Experience (30 secs)**
- Q30: Post-completion - clients complain? Redemption smooth or painful?
- Q31: Would you recommend to family? Do clients request them by name?

**Closing (30 secs)**
- Q32: One thing you'd change about this lender?
- Q33: Anything critical I didn't ask about?

**Important:**
- Record every interview (Zoom cloud recording with auto-transcript ON)
- Focus extra time on Q26-Q29 (war stories) - that's where the valuable insights are
- Keep interviews tight: 8-10 mins each
